Payment Information

In an attempt to keep the participant cost low, we preserved the registration fees from 2011:

Registration fee Received by March 30, {{ conf_year }} Received after March 30, {{ conf_year }}
Regular participants 450 USD 750 USD
Student participants 350 USD 550 USD
Accompanying persons (guests) 250 USD 350 USD

Accommodation is to be paid directly to the Stratosphere hotel. Please see the Conference Venue section for details.

The regular and student conference fees cover conference administration, on-site work of technicians, rental of conference facilities and audio-visual equipment, coffee breaks, welcome reception, preparation and printing of conference materials, and expenses of invited speakers. Each participant will receive a conference T-Shirt. The accompanying person (guest) fee covers coffee breaks, welcome reception, farewell party, and the conference T-Shirt.

Detailed payment instructions are available in your account after login.
