Post Conference Program

For participants wishing to spend the weekend after the conference in Prague, we will provide a tour of the Prague Old Town, Charles Bridge and Prague Castle on Saturday June 30. It is recommended to stay in the Angelo hotel in Pilsen until Saturday morning and take an early train to Prague. This takes approximately 2 hours. Then you can leave your baggage at the Main Railway Station in Prague. After touring Prague, you can pick up your bags and go directly to the Airport using the Airport Express bus. This bus goes approximately 50 minutes and it leaves every 30 minutes.
