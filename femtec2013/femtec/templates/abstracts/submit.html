Please enter your abstract below. Latex formulas are allowed, inclusion of images is not supported, and a few references to related or competitive work are required. If the format is correct, your abstract will be automatically forwarded for review. The review will be followed by a notification again. At any time, you can return to this page and check the current status of your submission.

Some Hints:

  • After filling the form below, click "Submit Abstract".
  • If your abstract compiles, please click on the PDF button and visually check the result. In particular, check the capitalization scheme of your title - First Letters of Important Words Should Be Capitalized.
  • If your abstract does not compile, please click on the LOG button and check the end of the log. There will be an error message. Since the error message refers to an auto-generated file, this will not immediately help you identify the problem. Make sure that you filled all required fields, and if you do not know what the error means, contact us and we will be happy to help. You do not need to send the log as we can view it in the database.
