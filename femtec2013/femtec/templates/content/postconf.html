Post Conference Program

For participants wishing to spend the weekend after the conference in Prague, we will provide a tour of the Prague Old Town, Charles Bridge and Prague Castle on Saturday June 30. A viable option is to stay at the Angelo hotel in Pilsen until Saturday morning and take an early train to Prague. One such train departs at 7:08 am and arrives at the Prague Main Station (PMS) at 8:51 am. Then you can leave your baggage at PMS. After touring Prague, you can pick up your bags and go either to your next hotel in Prague, or directly to the Airport using the Airport Express bus. This bus reaches the airport in approximately 50 minutes and it leaves PMS every 30 minutes. In any case, contact the organizers and we'll help with the arrangements.
