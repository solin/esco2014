Payment Information

In an attempt to keep the participant cost low, we preserved the registration fees from 2011:

Registration fee Received by March 15, 2013 Received after March 15, 2013
Regular participants 3450 USD 750 USD
Student participants 350 USD 550 USD
Accompanying persons (guests) 250 USD 350 USD

Estimated cost of accommodation is $500 for the 5 days of the conference. These will be either part of the registration fee or paid to the hotel separately (negotiations are still under way).

The regular and student conference fees cover conference administration, on-site work of technicians, rental of conference facilities and audio-visual equipment, coffee breaks, welcome reception, farewell party, preparation and printing of conference materials, and expenses of invited speakers. Each participant will receive a conference T-Shirt. The accompanying person (guest) fee covers coffee breaks, welcome reception, farewell party, and the conference T-Shirt.

Detailed payment instructions are available in your account after login.
